// About

The operator
behind the
strategy.

I started in journalism — interviewing, finding the angle, writing to deadline. I learned to listen hard and translate complexity without losing the human on the other side of it.

Then five years in the engine room of AI at Accenture, working on the data operations that make AI systems actually function — annotation pipelines, quality audits, model evaluation, SLA management for Fortune 10 clients. I saw where strategy collapses when nobody understands execution.

Journalism taught me to ask. Operations taught me to build. Parsons is teaching me to design the whole thing.

Now at Parsons (MS Strategic Design & Management, 4.0 GPA, class of 2026) — adding systems thinking, foresight, and impact entrepreneurship on top of the operator foundation. My thesis: leading research and prototype on Campus Belong, a pre-move-in onboarding platform for The New School's residence halls.

Alongside: market research at The Style That Binds Us, President of the Toastmasters Club at The New School, AIGA NY Applied Mentorship mentee, and member of intersection — a global community advancing climate and social justice through design.
